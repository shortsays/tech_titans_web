Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tech Titans? +
Tech Titans is a vibrant community dedicated to collaborative team projects and supporting newcomers in participating in hackathons, fostering innovation and skill development.
Why join Tech Titans? +
Joining Tech Titans provides access to a supportive network, opportunities to work on exciting projects, and guidance for first-time hackathon participants to enhance their technical and teamwork skills.
Does it support all languages? +
Tech Titans currently supports English, with plans to incorporate additional languages to make our community more inclusive and accessible.
